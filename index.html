Description

Welcome to “Shark Escape – Depths of the Ocean”!

Dive into the mysterious, treacherous world beneath the waves. You are Shark — swift, fearless, and ready for anything the deep throws at you. Your mission: survive the dangers of the ocean, uncover hidden treasures, and face the deadliest predator of all.

But beware — the deep sea is full of threats:

  • Jellyfish may move slowly, but one touch means instant death. Stay sharp and swim around them!
  • Pufferfish aren’t instantly lethal, but their poison drains your life bit by bit. Avoid them at all costs.
  • Barricades block your path — what hides beneath them? A deadly trap… or a hidden treasure? Only one way to find out.
  • And then, there’s the Final Boss: The Whale. Massive. Merciless. If you see it, RUN — or FIGHT! But be warned: you can only defeat the whale by shooting. Touch it, and you’re done for.
  • Poison Bottles: Collect these to enhance your attack power! They’re your best weapon against the Final Boss.
  • Coins: Gather as many as you can for extra rewards and bragging rights!

    • Are you ready to take on the deep? Swim, jump, fight — and survive!
